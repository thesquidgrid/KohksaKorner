CHOIR
Choir will be open to students of all grades.
For Choir, a good voice is welcome but
not mandatory. A good attitude to learn
and sing God's praises is what counts.
We are lucky to have 50 voices in our choir!
The Choir will sing Wednesday Masses,
Advent Mass, and on a very special
occasions.
BELL CHOIR
The Bell Choir is open to all students. The ability
to read music will be very helpful but not mandatory.
A good attitude and willingness to learn
is mandatory, however! We will play at
Wednesday Mass when we are prepared.
Hopefully, we will be ready to play at
our Advent Mass and for very special
occasions.
